About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a passionate freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. His love for the natural world drives his photography, capturing the rugged Scottish landscapes, native wildlife, and coastal birds in their most stunning moments.

With years of experience, Malcolm's expertise extends beyond landscape and wildlife photography. He also specializes in capturing the magic of weddings, creating timeless portraits, and documenting special events.

Explore Malcolm's portfolio and experience the beauty of Scotland through his lens.
